Rawal Samaj is a community organization based in Bangalore that brings together members of the Rawal community living across Bangalore and other parts of Karnataka. The society works as a supportive platform to connect families, preserve cultural values, and promote unity among its members. It organizes social, cultural, and community welfare activities for the benefit and development of the Rawal Samaj. Through gatherings, events, and mutual support initiatives, the organization helps strengthen relationships within the community and encourages cooperation and collective progress. Rawal Samaj plays an important role in creating a sense of belonging and shared identity among its members in the region.

Historical Significance

Traditionally, Brahmins were divided into several 'Gotras' and 'Shakhas' based on their lineage and the specific branch of Vedas they mastered. They served as advisors to kings (Rajpurohits) and played a pivotal role in shaping the legal, social, and religious fabric of ancient India.

From the philosophical depths of the Upanishads to the mathematical genius of Aryabhata, the community has consistently contributed to the global pool of human knowledge.
